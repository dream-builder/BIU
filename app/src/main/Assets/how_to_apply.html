Application Form is available at the Admission Office of the university and may be collected on payment of Tk. 300/-.

To get an Application Form by mail, one may write to the Office of the Registrar along with a Bank Draft/Pay Order of Tk. 300/- in favour of Bangladesh Islami University with a self-addressed envelope bearing postage stamp of Tk.12.

The completed Application Form should be submitted to the Admission Office with the following documents:

  1. Main or attested copies of all the academic certificates / mark sheets/transcripts
  2. Three copies of passport-size coloured photographs
  3. A certificate for break of study in case of irregular students
  4. Official transcripts for transferred students.
