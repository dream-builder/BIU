I heartily welcome you all to Bangladesh Islami University (BIU) website. I hope that your visit to this website will be useful and beneficial.

Bangladesh Islami University (BIU) was established in 2005 as a private university with the proper approval from the Government of the People’s Republic of Bangladesh to foster the intellectual, spiritual and physical development of the young learners and to make them able to attain excellence through the acquisition of knowledge and assimilation of moral values. The university imparts quality higher education to produce professional graduates capable of developing knowledge, self, society and nation. Since its inception in 2005, the university has been working efficiently and presently it has become a modern, dynamic and innovative institution providing high quality education in every branch of knowledge.

Bangladesh Islami University is flourishing rapidly and currently it has more than seven thousand students.

I extend my best wishes to the students and faculty members who are the most important part of our university and to those who seek to join us in our mission of sharing and creating knowledge.

I am sure you will find this website very useful and informative.

Prof. Dr. Anwarullah Chowdhury
Vice-Chancellor
