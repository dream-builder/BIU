For BBA, BA (Hons) and LLB (Hons) programs, an applicant should have at least second division both in SSC & HSC Exams or minimum GPA 2.50 in both the exams or equivalent exams (CGPA 5.00). But if any student gets at least 2.00 in any exam, he/she has to get total GPA at least 6.00 in both the exams.

An ‘O’ level student must have at least five subjects in ‘O’ level exam and an ‘A’ level student must have at least two subjects in ‘A’ level exam. Out of at least seven subjects of those ‘O’ level and ‘A’ level exams, the student must have B grade or GPA 4.0 in the exam, of four subjects and C grade or GPA 3.5 in remaining three subjects.

For MBA (Regular) program, a student should have at least a Bachelor’s degree. Experience may be taken as a plus point for admission.

For LL.M (Final) Program, a student should have at least LL.B (Hons) or must pass in LL.M (Preliminary) exam.

For LL.M (preliminary) Program, a student should have at least a degree LL.B (pass) from any recognized University.

For MA in Islamic Studies & in English, a student should have at least a Bachelor degree.

Admission is conducted by an Admission Committee formed by the Academic Council.

The candidates seeking admission to the above programs will have to sit for an admission test and obtain an acceptable score in that test. But the candidates who have scored at least 1100 in SAT, 450 in GMAT and 550 in TOEFL will be admitted directly to the BBA, LL.B (Hons) and BA (Hons) programs.
