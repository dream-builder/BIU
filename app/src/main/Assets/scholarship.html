BIU clearly visualizes that quality students blended with quality teaching are prerequisites for excellent education. So it offers a significant number of scholarships (BIU Trust scholarship, Vice-Chancellor’s scholarship, University scholarship & Departmental scholarship) and tuition fees waivers (full/partial) to attract the meritorious students.

Tuition fee Waiver

100% tuition fee waiver:
60% tuition fee waiver for CGPA: 5.0 in HSC and 40% tuition fee waiver for CGPA: 5.0 in SSC

70% tuition fee waiver:
40% tuition fee waiver for CGPA: 4.8 in HSC and 30% tuition fee waiver for CGPA: 4.8 in SSC

40% tuition fee waiver:
25% tuition fee waiver for CGPA: 4.5 in HSC and 15% tuition fee waiver for CGPA: 4.5 in SSC

20% tuition fee waiver:
15% tuition fee waiver for CGPA: 3.5 in HSC and 05% tuition fee waiver for CGPA: 3.5 in SSC

Special Waiver
There are special waivers of tuition fees for spouse, sibling, tribal, boys’ scout & girls’ guide, and the wards of freedom fighters. All waivers are applicable for regular undergraduate students (who passed SSC not exceeding 5 years ago & HSC not exceeding 2 years ago) excluding optional subject.
